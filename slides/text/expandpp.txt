--- PAGE 19 ---
Example
Requests arrive to a web server in the following manner:
Requests for domain a.com according to a Poisson process
with rate four per minute, while requests for b.com arrive
according to a Poisson process with rate six per minute. The
two processes are independent.
a) What is the expected time of the next arrival to the server?
b) If 10 arrivals occurred to the system in one minute, what is
the probability that exactly four of them are for a.com?
c) The last a.com arrival was 10 seconds ago. The last b.com
arrival was 15 seconds ago. What is the expected time to
the next a.com arrival?
