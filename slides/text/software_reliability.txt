--- PAGE 2 ---
Characteristics of Software Reliability
• Failures are primarily due to design faults
• Repairs/fixes reduce the possibility of future failure

• There is no wear-out phenomena
• Upgrades can result in reduced failure rates
• External environment does not affect software reliability
• Internal environment (memory size, clock speed, etc.) can affect
software reliability

• Reliability is not time dependent (in a strict sense)
• Depends on testing time
• Reliability growth is observed as errors are detected and corrected
