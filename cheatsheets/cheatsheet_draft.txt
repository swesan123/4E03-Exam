Common queue + exponential service + multiple servers → M/M/k
If asking “probability an arriving job waits before service starts” → this is probability of queueing P_Q,
Given mean processing/service time E[S], first convert to service rate: μ = 1 / E[S]
Let C = λ / μ
Per-server utilization with k servers: ρ = λ / (kμ) = C / k
Use when solving part (a):
p0 = ( Σ from j=0 to k-1 of (C^j / j!) + C^k / (k!(1-ρ)) )^(-1)
P_Q = p0 * C^k / (k!(1-ρ))
Method for part (a): try k = 1, 2, 3, ... until P_Q is below target; choose the smallest such k

Blocking probability = π_U
Throughput = λ(1 - π_U)
Probability a job waits = Σ from n=1 to U-1 of π_n = 1 - π_0 - π_U
Method for part (b): choose the largest feasible U that keeps P(wait) below target, since larger U gives less blocking and higher throughput
