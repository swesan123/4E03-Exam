\section*{3.\ Introduction to queueing}
\textbf{System.}\ Arrivals (distribution of inter-arrivals);\ queue (finite/$\infty$);\ server(s);\ discipline (FCFS, LCFS, $\ldots$);\ service-time distribution.\\
\textbf{Metrics.}\ Response time $E[T]$ (arrival$\to$departure);\ throughput $X$ (completions/time).\\
\textbf{Notation.}\ $\lambda$ arrival rate;\ $\mu$ service rate (per server);\ $\rho$ utilization;\ $E[W]$ queue wait;\ $E[S]$ service;\ $p_n$ or $\pi_n$ length probs.
