\section*{11.\ Reliability}
\textbf{Lifetime $X$.}\ cdf $F$;\ pdf $f$;\ survival $1-F$.\\
\textbf{Hazard.}\ $h(x)=f(x)/(1-F(x))$ (instantaneous failure rate).\\
\textbf{IFR / DFR.}\ $h$ nondecreasing / nonincreasing.\\
\textbf{Constant $h$.}\ $\Leftrightarrow$ exponential lifetime.\\
\textbf{CTMC view.}\ Constant failure/repair rates $\Rightarrow$ availability via CTMC (balance).\\
\textbf{MTTF (transient).}\ First-passage equations (avoid steady-state on absorbing chain).
