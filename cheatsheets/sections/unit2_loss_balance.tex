\section*{Loss / no-wait (two-state)}
\textbf{Model:} 1 server, no wait; arrivals lost if busy.\\
$\pi_0$ idle,\ $\pi_1$ busy;\quad $\mu_{\mathrm{eff}}=1/E[S]$.\\
Mix types: $E[S]=\sum_i p_i E[S_i]$;\quad $\mu_{\mathrm{eff}}=1/E[S]$.\\
Balance: $\lambda\pi_0=\mu_{\mathrm{eff}}\pi_1$;\quad $\pi_0=1-\pi_1$ $\Rightarrow$
$\pi_1=\lambda/(\lambda+\mu_{\mathrm{eff}})$ (busy prob).
