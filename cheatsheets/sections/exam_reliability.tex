\section*{Reliability (lifetime / hazard)}
$h(x)=f(x)/(1-F(x))$;\quad IFR: $h$ $\uparrow$;\quad DFR: $h$ $\downarrow$.\\
$F(x)=\Prob(X\le x)$;\quad $1-F(x)=\Prob(X>x)$.\\
Normalize $c$: $\int_a^b f(x)\,dx=1$ (limits from support).\\
$F(x)=\int_{\text{left}}^x f(t)\,dt$ (match domain).\\
IFR/DFR: classify via $h(x)$.
